About Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. The story follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who dreams of becoming the strongest ninja in his village.

The series has been hugely popular since its debut in 1999, and has spawned multiple anime series, movies, and video games.
